State of California ‚Äì Department of Cannabis Control (DCC)

This section summarizes the main state license categories. Local (county/city) approval is usually required before or alongside state licensure.

Retailer (Storefront & Delivery)
Customer-facing sales ‚Äì physical premises and/or delivery.
License: Retailer
  • Retail store, dispensary, or delivery-only operations.
  • Requires compliant premises, security, and local authorization.
  • Subject to advertising, packaging, and sales restrictions.
Open DCC retailer guidance ‚ü∂
Microbusiness
Small, vertically integrated operations.
License: Microbusiness
  • Combines at least three activities (e.g., cultivation, manufacturing, distribution, retail).
  • Requires compliance with all rules for each selected activity.
  • Often subject to additional local caps and zoning limits.
Open microbusiness requirements ‚ü∂
Cultivation
Outdoor, indoor, mixed-light, nursery, processor.
License: Cultivation
  • Multiple license types based on canopy size and lighting.
  • Includes nursery and processor categories.
  • Requires CEQA compliance, water/energy, and environmental review.
View cultivation license matrix ‚ü∂
Manufacturing
Non-volatile, volatile, infusion, packaging & labeling.
License: Manufacturing
  • Type 6 (non-volatile), Type 7 (volatile), and other categories.
  • Requires robust SOPs, safety procedures, and facility approvals.
  • Subject to product testing, packaging, and labeling standards.
View manufacturing guidance ‚ü∂
Distribution
Transport, storage, quality assurance, tax collection.
License: Distributor
  • Moves cannabis products between licensees.
  • Performs quality assurance reviews and testing coordination.
  • Responsible for certain taxes and track-and-trace entries.
View distribution requirements ‚ü∂
Testing Laboratory
Independent product testing & COAs.
License: Testing Laboratory
  • Performs required compliance testing for cannabis goods.
  • Requires ISO accreditation and rigorous QA systems.
  • Issue Certificates of Analysis (COAs) for product batches.
Open lab requirements ‚ü∂
